# 🛡️ Phishing Email Analysis

## 📧 Sample Email Summary
- From: `security-alert@micros0ft-support.com`
- Subject: Urgent – Suspicious Activity Detected
- Link: `https://microsoft-support-login.com/verify`

## 🚩 Phishing Signs Detected
- Spoofed domain (`micros0ft-support.com` instead of microsoft.com)
- Suspicious link with misleading display
- Generic greeting (“Dear user”)
- Threatening language to scare user
- Grammar and formatting issues

## ✅ Conclusion
This is a phishing email trying to trick the recipient into clicking a fake link to steal login credentials.
